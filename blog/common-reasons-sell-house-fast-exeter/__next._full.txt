Months of effort are wasted, and you are back to square one.</p><p>Chain collapse is common in Exeter's market. The interconnected nature of most property transactions, where each sale depends on another, creates fragile structures that are vulnerable to problems at any link. A buyer's mortgage being declined in Pinhoe can collapse a chain that stretches through St Thomas, Heavitree, and Topsham.</p><p>After a chain collapse, many Exeter homeowners choose to sell to a cash buyer rather than risk the same experience again. We purchase properties in any state of repair, from cosmetically tired to structurally compromised.
